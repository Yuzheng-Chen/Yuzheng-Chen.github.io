Hi! I’m Yuzheng Chen, a Master by Research student at Lancaster University, supervised by Hans Gellersen in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). My research covers various HCI projects, from VR/AR interaction techniques to Electric Muscle Simulation (EMS) games. Right now, I’m working on defining my research focus and actively exploring PhD opportunities. Feel free to reach out if you’d like to connect!

  • Undergraduate researcher at XJTLU’s X-CHI Lab, supervised by Hai-Ning Liang.
  • Research intern with Florian 'Floyd' Mueller at Monash University’s Exertion Games Lab in 2022.
  • Research intern at the Computational Media and Arts (CMA) Lab at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou) in 2023.
