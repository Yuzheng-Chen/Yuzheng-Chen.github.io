Roc Serpent Drama

Transport yourself to a world of myth and legend with this captivating game, where the legendary roc serpent takes center stage. Follow the serpent's transformation from humble beginnings to powerful dragon, all against a stunning backdrop of traditional Chinese scenery.

With easy-to-learn action strategy gameplay, players take on the role of the roc serpent, consuming divine pills to increase their length and evolve through the ravages of thunderstorms, all while racing against the clock to earn the highest score.

With compact game design and short playtime, it's perfect for challenging your friends in a quick match, weaving through the clouds and putting your reflexes and memory to the test. Are you ready to join the journey and become a legendary roc serpent?
