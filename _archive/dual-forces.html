Dual Forces - The Battle for Yin and Yang

Welcome to the world of "Yin Yang Battle", a game made in the Global Game Jam 2022, with the theme of "Duality". In this exciting game, you will experience the power of Yin and Yang, and battle against various types of monsters that possess unique skills.

As a player, you must maintain a delicate balance between your Yin and Yang powers while using them to attack your enemies. You have three types of attacks at your disposal: Normal attack, Yin attack, and Yang attack. Each attack has its own unique effects on the Yin and Yang values of the player, which may influence the attack points.

Your ultimate goal is to defeat all the monsters that appear on your path, each with different strengths and weaknesses. However, to succeed, you must manage your Yin and Yang levels carefully to maintain the balance of power.

Are you ready to take on the challenge of "Yin Yang Battle"? Get ready to use your strategic skills and harness the power of Yin and Yang to emerge victorious!
